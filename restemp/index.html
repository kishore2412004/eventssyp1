Box-Cricket

Description : where cricket is more than just a sport, it is no surprise that even its informal variations hold a special place in people's hearts. Box cricket, a fast-paced and exciting cousin of the traditional game, thrives in streets, terraces, and even specially designed indoor arenas across the country.

Rules & Regulations

  • A Team has 5 players, will play the game.

  • Event will take place in volley ball court and ball badminton court , hitting 6 will be considered as out .
